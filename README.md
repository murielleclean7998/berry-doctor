# 🍓 berry-doctor - Simple tools for healthy strawberry crops

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_BerryDoctor-blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/murielleclean7998/berry-doctor/releases)

BerryDoctor helps farmers monitor strawberry health using Kakao. The software identifies plant diseases, provides weather alerts, and offers coaching for your farm. This tool assists you in making informed decisions about your crops.

## 📥 Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up the software on your Windows computer.

1. Go to the [official release page](https://github.com/murielleclean7998/berry-doctor/releases).
2. Look for the latest version at the top of the list.
3. Click the file ending in .exe to start your download.
4. Save the file to a folder you can find later, such as your Downloads folder.

## ⚙️ System Requirements

Your computer needs these to run the software:

* Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* At least 4 gigabytes of RAM.
* A stable internet connection for weather updates and Kakao messages.
* Enough space on your drive for saved logs and images.

## 🛠️ Installation

Once the download finishes, follow these instructions to install the program:

1. Open your Downloads folder.
2. Double-click the file you downloaded.
3. A Windows security window might appear. Click "More info" and then click "Run anyway" if the system asks.
4. Follow the prompts on the screen to finish the setup.
5. A shortcut icon will appear on your desktop. Double-click it to start the program.

## 🌾 Using the App

The main screen shows your farm status. You can upload photos of your strawberries to check for plant diseases. The app uses the camera or existing files to look for signs of illness.

The dashboard displays weather patterns in your local area. The app sends alerts if frost or extreme heat threatens your strawberries. 

The coaching tab contains tips for your daily tasks. Use these tips to manage soil nutrients and plant spacing.

## 💬 Kakao Integration

BerryDoctor sends updates directly to your Kakao account. 

1. Go to the settings menu in the app.
2. Select the Kakao integration tab.
3. Click the button to link your account.
4. Follow the pop-up window to approve the request.
5. Close the window and save your settings.

You now receive notifications on your phone whenever the diagnostic tool finishes an analysis or the weather changes.

## ℹ️ Troubleshooting

If the software does not open:

* Check your internet connection.
* Restart your computer.
* Uninstall the app and download it again from the [release page](https://github.com/murielleclean7998/berry-doctor/releases). 
* Make sure your Windows version is up to date.

If the camera tool fails to detect a disease:

* Capture a clear image in good light.
* Ensure the leaf stays in the center of the frame.
* Clean your camera lens if the image looks blurry.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Safety

This software keeps your farm data on your computer. You choose when to send photos for diagnostic analysis. The app only uses the information provided to give you farming advice. We do not sell your data or share your personal details with others. 

## ❓ Common Questions

**How does the app find diseases?**
The software compares your photos against a library of healthy and sick plants. It tells you the most likely condition based on what it sees.

**Can I use the app without the internet?**
The app needs the internet to send Kakao notifications and get current weather reports. You can view past logs without a web connection.

**Does it track my location?**
The app needs your location to provide accurate weather alerts for your farm. This information stays private and only helps provide local updates.

**How often should I check the app?**
Check once every morning to see the weather forecast. Check after you walk through your farm to log any issues with plant growth. 

**What if the app freezes?**
Close the program and wait ten seconds. Open it again. If the issue keeps happening, reach out to your support contact.

**Can I run this on a tablet?**
This software works on Windows tablets that meet the system requirements. It does not work on iPads or Android tablets.

**Are there subscription fees?**
The Phase 0 version is free to use. 

**Will this work for other crops?**
This version focuses on strawberries. We may add support for other plants in the future. 

**How do I update the software?**
When a new version comes out, return to the release page. Replace your current file with the updated version. Your settings usually stay the same after an update.
